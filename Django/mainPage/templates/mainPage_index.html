Artificial Intelligence Home

Welcome to the Artificial Intelligence assignment made by Sybrand van Loenen and Emyl van der Kooi. This assignment is focussing on making predictions based on questions from the Mental Health in the Tech Industry survey. In order to predict a specific outcome of a question, a survey is made that can be filled in by anyone.

Where can I find the survey?

You are able to access the survey by clicking on the Survey button on the sidebar. The survey consists of twelve questions, all of them are from the Mental Health in the Tech Industry survey. The dataset can be found here: Mental Health in the Tech Industry. After the survey has been filled in by the employees, the employer can view the overall results on the results page.

Where can I find the results of the survey?

The results are accessible by clicking the results button in the sidebar. If this website is used by a company, the page will only be accessible by the employer. But for now, it is accessible for everyone.

Please feel free to look around and if you are interested in the code, it is stored on GitHub. Simply click on the GitHub button in the sidebar.
